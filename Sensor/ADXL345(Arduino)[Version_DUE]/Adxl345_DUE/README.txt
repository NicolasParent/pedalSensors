Adaptadted version of the ADXL345 library for Arduino DUE from pesalSensors team

based in "masc" post in http://forum.bildr.org/viewtopic.php?f=29&t=349&start=75

Arduino Due should be connected as follows:

ADXL345					Arduino DUE
3.3V	-----------------------------	3.3V
GND	-----------------------------	GND
SCL	-----------------------------	SCL 21
SDA	-----------------------------	SDA 20
